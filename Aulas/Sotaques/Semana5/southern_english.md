# Southern English

- started abt mid 20th century
  - reaction to influx of northerners post-WWII
- monophthongization of [ai]
  - some speakers only _# or _[+voiced]
  - starting to spread to \_[-voiced
- Southern shift
  - i <-> ɪ, e <-> ɛ
  - /eɪ/ [әe]
  - /ɛ/ [eә]
  - /i/ [ɨi]
  - /ɪ/ [iә]
- fronting of /u/
  - shared w west
  - /o/ fronts as well (S only
- pin/pen merger
